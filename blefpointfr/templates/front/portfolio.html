WIS

WIS is a mysterious tool. WIS allow people to share pictures around the web easily by offering a simple register process and an pure interface. But the concept of WIS doesn't lie in a simple sharing, WIS is also a open-source app for Django developers who are looking for an gallery application. Moreover the gallery view of WIS is unique! For this tool I have worked in collaboration with Franck and it was a great experience. We want to improve WIS in the future to allow users to choose their own storage.

http://www.wis.fr
Stack : Javascript - Django - SASS

tdlst

When you are a developer you are continuously looking for an idea. But sometimes a simple thing can give an idea. A day I needed a todolist but I don't find a web hosted todolist fitted for me. So I decided to implements mine, and tdlst was born. With tdlst you can share tdlst with your friends, your friends can subscribe to your tdlst and never forget something. And remember, don't forget the vowel.

http://www.tdlst.fr
Stack : Websockets - Coffeescript - Django - SASS
